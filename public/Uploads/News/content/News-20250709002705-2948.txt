																						</p><p><span style="font-size: inherit;">DUMAGUETE CITY, Negros Oriental—Ten awards were conferred to Central Philippines State University (CPSU) during the 1<i>st</i> ever Commission on Higher Education Region XVIII-Negros Island Region (CHED RO XVIII-NIR) Regional Quality Awards.</span></p><p>&nbsp;</p><p><span style="font-size: inherit;">CPSU was given the following awards:</span></p><ol><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">Recognition for the Provision to CHED RO XVIII-NIR Endeavors</span></li><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">Recognition for State Universities and Colleges (SUCs) with 100% Certificate of Program Compliance (COPCs) of Undergraduate and Graduate Programs</span></li><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">Award for Excellence in Community Extension</span></li><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">Award for Excellence in Research</span></li><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">Award for Excellence in Internationalization Initiatives</span></li><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">Model Implementer Award on timeliness, completeness, and accuracy of data submitted to CHED RO XVIII-NIR Higher Education Information Management System (HEMIS)</span></li><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">World Rankings (Quacquarelli Symonds World University Rankings—Asia—3 Stars)</span></li><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">World Rankings (ASEAN University Network-Quality Assurance / AUN-QA)</span></li><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">World Rankings (World University Rankings for Innovation / WURI)</span></li><li><span style="font-size: inherit;">&nbsp;World Rankings (The Times Higher Education / THE)</span></li></ol><p>&nbsp;</p><p><span style="font-size: inherit;">The certificates of recognition were presented by CHED RO VXIII-NIR Regional Director, Dr. Raul C. Alavarez, Jr., REE, Ed.D., CESO III, to awardees during the awarding ceremony held at the Luce Auditorium, Silliman University, on May 30, 2025.</span></p><p>&nbsp;</p><p><span style="font-size: inherit;">Newly appointed CHED Chairperson, Hon. Shirley C. Agrupis, Ph.D., and CHED Commissioner, Hon. Desiderio R. Fordente (Victorias Campus).</span></p><p>&nbsp;</p><p><span data-teams="true"></span></p><p><span style="font-size: inherit;">At present Region XVIII has 88 HEIs spread throughout the provinces of Negros Occidental, Negros Oriental, and Siquijor.</span></p>
